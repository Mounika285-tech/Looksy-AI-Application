# LookSy - AI-Powered Digital Wardrobe & Smart Fashion Assistant

## App Overview

LookSy is a mobile application that acts as a personal AI fashion stylist and digital wardrobe organizer.

The app allows users to digitally store their clothes, footwear, accessories, watches, jewelry, handbags, and other fashion items in one place. Using AI, the app helps users generate outfit recommendations based on their wardrobe, weather conditions, occasions, color matching, and style preferences.

The main goal is to help users choose outfits faster, use existing clothes more effectively, and maintain a well-organized digital wardrobe.

# Core Features

## 1. Digital Wardrobe

* Jackets
* Dresses

* Bags

* Category
* Color
* Pattern
* Season
* Occasion

Users can review and edit details before saving.

---

## 2. AI Outfit Generator

AI creates outfits using only items available in the user's wardrobe.

Features:

### Mix & Match

User selects one or more wardrobe items.

Examples:

* Shirt
* Pant
* Shoes
* Watch

AI suggests matching remaining items and generates a complete outfit.

### Weather Styling

Automatically fetch weather using user location.

Examples:

* Sunny
* Rainy
* Cloudy
* Cold

AI recommends suitable outfits from the user's wardrobe.

### Occasion Styling

User selects or enters an occasion.

Preset options:

* Wedding
* Office
* College
* Party
* Date
* Festival
* Casual Outing

AI generates a complete outfit suitable for the occasion.

### Color Matching

User selects a clothing item.

AI recommends matching:

* Pants
* Shoes
* Accessories
* Watches

based on color harmony and style compatibility.

### AI Recommended Planner

AI generates outfit plans for:

Users receive notifications and reminders.
